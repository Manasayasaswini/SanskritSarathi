Sanskrit Sarathi is an AI-powered pedagogical platform designed to master the "Science of Sound." 
Unlike standard language apps that prioritize vocabulary, Sanskrit Sarathi focuses on Shiksha (Phonetics)—ensuring users achieve the exact mouth placement (Sthana) and effort (Prayatna) required for Vedic and Classical Sanskrit.

The tool acts as a "Digital Charioteer," guiding the student through:

Acoustic Precision: Real-time analysis of aspirated vs. non-aspirated consonants.

Prosody & Meter: Correcting the duration ($Matras$) of short ($Hrasva$) and long ($Dirgha$) vowels.

Contextual Breaking: Deconstructing complex $Sandhi$ (phonetic transitions) for easier chanting.
