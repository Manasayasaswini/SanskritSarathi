# Project Scope: Sanskrit Sarathi (संस्कृत सारथि)

## In-Scope (Phase 1: MVP)

The Varna Matrix: Interactive chart for all 50+ Sanskrit characters with AI feedback on each.

Syllable-Level Scoring: Visual highlighting (Green/Red) of individual letters within a word.

Transliteration Engine: Support for Devanagari, IAST (Roman), and ITRANS.

Basic Shloka Library: 10 essential verses (e.g., Shanti Mantras) for guided practice.


## In-Scope (Phase 2: Intermediate)

Sandhi-Viccheda Tool: Automated breaking of long compound words into readable parts.

Progress Dashboard: Tracking "Phonetic Accuracy" over time.

Leaderboards: Encouraging community practice through "Chanting Streaks."


## Future Development: Deep Grammar Analysis 

Complex $Vyakaran$ (morphology) is secondary to pronunciation in the MVP.

Handwriting Recognition: OCR for ancient manuscripts (planned for Phase 3).

Real-time Human Tutoring: The goal is a purely AI-driven "Sarathi" initially.


# Sanskrit Sarathi: Project Roadmap

## 🎯 Phase 1: The Foundation (MVP)
**Focus:** Phonetic Accuracy & Basic Literacy
*   **The Varna Matrix:** Interactive chart for all 50+ Sanskrit characters.
    *   **AI Feedback:** Instant "Match Score" for individual sounds.
    *   **Mouth Mechanic Guides:** Simple diagrams showing tongue placement (e.g., Kanthya vs. Dantya).
*   **Syllable-Level Scoring:** Visual highlighting (Green/Yellow/Red) of letters within a word.
*   **Transliteration Engine:** One-tap toggle between Devanagari, IAST, and ITRANS.
*   **Basic Shloka Library:** 10 essential verses with Slow-Mo playback (0.5x, 0.75x).

## 📈 Phase 2: The Journey (Intermediate)
**Focus:** Flow, Connection, and Retention
*   **Sandhi-Viccheda Tool:** Automated breaking of long compound words into readable, individual parts.
*   **Anusvara & Visarga Tracker:** Specialized AI detection for the subtle nasal and breathy endings.
*   **Progress Dashboard:** Visualizing "Accuracy Growth" and "Mastered Varnas."
*   **Chanting Streaks:** Gamified daily reminders and leaderboards to build a practice habit.

## 🔮 Phase 3: Future Horizons (Advanced)
**Focus:** Deep Mastery & Digital Preservation
*   **Deep Grammar Analysis:** Breaking down word roots ($Dhatus$) and case endings ($Vibhakti$) using LLM logic.
*   **Manuscript OCR:** Use the camera to scan ancient texts/manuscripts and hear the AI pronounce them instantly.
*   **Heritage Mode (Voice Cloning):** Allow users to upload a recording of a specific Guru or elder to serve as the "Target Voice" for the AI to compare against.
*   **Real-time Human Tutoring:** An integrated marketplace to connect with live Sanskrit experts for advanced certification.

## Summary Checklist for Dev Start:
*   **Speech API:** Bhashini or OpenAI Whisper (Fine-tuned).
*   **Logic:** Phonetic distance algorithm for scoring.
*   **UI:** Clean, distraction-free interface with a "Saffron/Traditional" aesthetic.
